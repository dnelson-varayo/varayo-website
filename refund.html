General Provisions −

All services provided by Varayo Inc. ("Varayo," "we," "us," or "our") are subject to the following refund and cancellation policies. By subscribing to or utilizing our services, you agree to these terms.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise in a separate Service Level Agreement (SLA) or custom contract, all fees are generally non-refundable.

  • Non-Refundable Fees: Setup fees, installation charges, one-time equipment purchases, domain registration fees, and prepaid annual service fees are non-refundable.
  • Prorated Refunds (if applicable): Monthly service fees are generally not eligible for prorated refunds upon cancellation, except where required by state or federal law.
  • SLA Credits: If an outage occurs that violates the uptime guarantees outlined in your specific Service Level Agreement (SLA), you may be eligible for service credits, which are not cash refunds.
Fiber Internet Services (Early Termination Fees Apply) +

Fiber internet services require a contractual commitment (typically 12, 24, or 36 months) to secure preferential pricing, installation subsidies, and dedicated resources.

No Money-Back Guarantee

Due to the significant upfront investment in design, permitting, construction, and installation of dedicated fiber circuits, no refunds are available once the service agreement is signed.

Billing and Cancellation

Billing begins upon completion and activation of the circuit (service ready for use). Once billing has commenced, the service cannot be canceled without penalty for the remainder of the contract term.

Early Termination Fee (ETF)

If you terminate service before the end of the contract term, an Early Termination Fee (ETF) will apply. The ETF typically covers the remaining months of the contract (prorated or as specified in your agreement) plus recovery of any waived or subsidized installation, construction, activation, or equipment costs.

Service continues until the end of the current billing cycle following formal cancellation notice, and you remain responsible for all charges during that period. Month-to-month options are not available for fiber internet services.

Cancellation Process +

To cancel any Varayo Inc. service, the following procedure must be followed:

  • Notification Requirement: All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing (via email to [email protected] or through a formal support ticket) by the authorized account holder.
  • Effective Date: Cancellation requests are processed at the end of the current billing cycle. Services remain active until the end of the paid cycle.
  • Terminated Accounts: If the service is under a term commitment, an early termination fee will be assessed and must be paid before the account can be fully closed.
  • Final Bill: You will receive a final bill that may include any outstanding usage fees, termination charges, or equipment return fees.
VoIP Services +

VoIP services are provided on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contract required.

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

New VoIP activations qualify for a 30-day money-back guarantee. If dissatisfied within the first 30 days after activation, contact us to cancel and receive a refund or credit for standard monthly service fees paid (excluding taxes, surcharges, one-time setup fees, or equipment purchases).

Cancellation After 30 Days

Provide written notice (email acceptable). Service continues until the end of the current billing cycle; no prorated refunds for partial months.

Prepaid credits or unused balances may be refunded upon request, minus any outstanding fees.

Equipment Purchases and Returns +

New equipment purchases (VoIP Phones, Routers, Switches, etc.) include a 30-day return window from the delivery date.

  • Return unused items in original packaging and condition (including all accessories, manuals, and packaging) for a full refund, minus shipping costs.
  • A restocking fee (up to 15-20%) may apply to opened or used items at our discretion.
  • Refurbished or custom-configured equipment is final sale and non-returnable/non-refundable.
  • Defective equipment under warranty follows the manufacturer’s policy (repair or replacement, not refund).
  • Shipping costs for returns are the customer’s responsibility unless the item is defective or shipped in error.
  • Software, licenses, and digital products are non-refundable.
Exceptions and Special Circumstances +

Refunds may be considered only under the following specific circumstances:

  • Billing Errors: If you were erroneously double-charged or billed incorrectly, a refund for the excess amount will be processed immediately upon verification by our billing team.
  • Service Outages (SLA): If a service outage exceeds the guaranteed uptime specified in your Service Level Agreement (SLA), you may be eligible for a service credit or refund as defined in that SLA. Eligibility is subject to verification of the outage metrics.
  • Hardware Failure: If Varayo-rented or Varayo-provided equipment fails and we are unable to replace it within a reasonable timeframe, a credit may be applied for the period the service was unusable.
Payment & Security Details +

Varayo Inc. accepts payments via ACH, Credit Card, and wire transfer. All credit card transactions are processed securely through PCI DSS compliant third-party processors. Varayo Inc. does not store full credit card information on its servers.

Automatic recurring billing is the default method for most services. Customers are responsible for keeping payment information current to avoid service interruption due to failed payments.
