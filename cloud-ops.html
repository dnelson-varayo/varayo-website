Operations scope

What we handle

Ongoing operational responsibilities that keep environments stable, secure, and cost-effective.

Availability monitoring
Continuous health checks, alerting, and incident response procedures. We detect issues before they impact users and follow escalation paths to resolve them.
Patching & updates
Routine security updates with scheduled maintenance windows. We test patches in staging before production deployment and maintain rollback procedures.
Security posture
Baseline hardening, change controls, and regular security assessments. We enforce least privilege access and maintain audit logs.
Cost optimization
Right-sizing resources, eliminating waste, and providing visibility into spending. We identify idle resources and recommend architectural changes that reduce costs.
