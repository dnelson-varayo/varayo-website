Core cloud hosting capabilities

Flexible infrastructure building blocks, with architecture and managed operations — so your team can focus on the business.

Virtual Data Center (VDC)
Build your private cloud with dedicated resources and true isolation — ideal for critical ERP systems, high-demand databases, and latency-sensitive workloads.
Bare Metal & Dedicated Servers
Single-tenant physical hardware with full root access. Customize CPU, RAM, and storage for maximum performance, licensing flexibility, or strict compliance environments.
Hybrid Cloud Integration
Securely connect on‑prem to our cloud, or link directly to AWS/Azure. We architect private connectivity for consistent performance across your hybrid environment.
24/7/365 Managed Support
Our infrastructure is monitored around the clock by LA‑based experts. Support includes monitoring, patching, maintenance, and rapid escalation for maximum uptime.
Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS)
Geo‑redundant replication, automated backups, and failover planning to achieve aggressive RTO/RPO objectives and keep operations online during incidents.
Enterprise Security Suite
Security built into infrastructure: DDoS mitigation, NGFW options, endpoint protection, and continuous vulnerability scanning to protect your assets.
High-Speed Network & Fiber
Hosting backed by Varayo’s low‑latency fiber network. Faster data transfer, quicker app response, and stronger performance for global users and distributed teams.
Data Sovereignty & Compliance
Maintain control over residency for regulatory needs (HIPAA, PCI‑DSS, GDPR). We provide transparent, audited zones and architecture aligned to your compliance requirements.
