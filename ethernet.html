Enterprise Layer 2 connectivity

Varayo’s Ethernet Private Line (EPL) and Ethernet Virtual Private Line (EVPL) services deliver cost-effective, high-bandwidth transport between sites with guaranteed capacity and predictable latency — ideal for critical applications.

Ethernet Private Line (EPL)
Dedicated, point-to-point service offering maximum isolation and transparency between two locations.
Ethernet Virtual Private Line (EVPL)
Multipoint (hub-and-spoke) connectivity — ideal for connecting headquarters to multiple branch offices.
Scalable Bandwidth
Scale from 10 Mbps up to 100 Gbps — pay only for the capacity you need, with clear upgrade paths.
Secure & Private
Private Layer 2 transport with strong separation from the public internet — ideal for sensitive data movement and compliance.
