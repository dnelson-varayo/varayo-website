Email options for every team

We specialize in Microsoft 365 (our primary recommendation), while also supporting Hosted Exchange and Google Workspace based on your workflows, compliance needs, and budget.

Microsoft 365 (Recommended)

The leading productivity suite for modern business — email + collaboration + security.

  • Hosted email via Outlook with 50GB+ mailboxes
  • Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive (1TB+)
  • Advanced security: threat protection, DLP, MFA
  • Best-in-class collaboration with Teams

Hosted Exchange

Classic Exchange functionality without the full Microsoft 365 suite.

  • Shared calendars, contacts, tasks, and GAL
  • Large mailboxes (50–100GB+ options)
  • Anti-spam, anti-malware, and archiving options
  • Flexible plans with optional add-ons

Google Workspace

A cloud-first alternative with intuitive collaboration tools.

  • Business Gmail with 30GB+ options
  • Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Meet, Calendar
  • Strong phishing protection and admin controls
  • Great for real-time editing workflows
Pricing varies by plan, user count, and selected features — contact us for a customized quote.
